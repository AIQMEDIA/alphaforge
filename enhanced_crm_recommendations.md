# Enhanced CRM System for Trade Desk Detection & IP Protection

## Executive Summary

I've enhanced AlphaForge's CRM system with advanced lead intelligence capabilities specifically designed to identify trade desk entities while protecting against IP infringement and bad actors. This system provides comprehensive competitive intelligence while maintaining professional lead capture.

## Key Enhancements Added

### 1. Advanced Trade Desk Detection Schema
**New CRM Lead Fields:**
- `companyType`: Categorizes leads (prop_trading, hedge_fund, trade_desk, market_maker, etc.)
- `businessModel`: B2B vs B2C identification
- `tradingVolume`: Estimated trading volume brackets
- `primaryAssets`: Asset class focus areas
- `tradeDeskDetected`: Boolean flag for confirmed trade desks

### 2. Behavioral Analytics for IP Protection
**User Behavior Tracking:**
- `pageViews`: Total page interactions
- `timeOnSite`: Engagement duration
- `featuresViewed`: Array of specific features accessed
- `downloadedContent`: Content consumption patterns
- `suspiciousPatterns`: Automated red flag detection

### 3. Technical Fingerprinting System
**Device & Browser Intelligence:**
- `browserFingerprint`: Unique device identification
- `deviceType`: Desktop, mobile, tablet classification
- `operatingSystem`: OS detection
- `screenResolution`: Display characteristics
- `timezone` & `language`: Geographic indicators

### 4. Competitive Intelligence Tracking
**Traffic Source Analysis:**
- `referrerDomain`: Incoming traffic sources
- `utmSource/Medium/Campaign`: Marketing attribution
- `searchKeywords`: SEO competitive intelligence
- `competitorRiskScore`: 0-100 automated scoring

### 5. Bad Actor Detection System
**Risk Assessment Fields:**
- `badActorScore`: Automated threat scoring
- `ipInfringementFlags`: Specific suspicious indicators
- `suspiciousPatterns`: Behavioral anomaly detection

## Lead Intelligence Service Features

### Automatic Trade Desk Detection
**Company Analysis:**
- Scans company names for trade desk keywords
- Identifies prop trading firms, hedge funds, market makers
- Detects competitive entities (QuantConnect, Interactive Brokers, etc.)
- Flags suspicious email domains

**Risk Scoring Algorithm:**
- 25 points: Trade desk keywords in company name
- 40 points: Known competitor companies
- 50 points: Suspicious email domains
- 15 points: Trading-related job titles
- Additional points for behavioral patterns
