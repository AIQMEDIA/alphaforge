## Recommended Testing Approach

### Phase 1: Paper Trading Validation (Start Here)
**Goal**: Test all features risk-free with virtual money

**Strategy**: Start with $10,000 virtual portfolio
1. **Quantum Portfolio Optimization**
   - Test IBM Quantum VQE algorithm with tech stocks (AAPL, GOOGL, MSFT)
   - Compare Google Cirq QAOA results with traditional optimization
   - Track Amazon Braket quantum ML predictions vs market performance

2. **Risk Management Testing**
   - Set position limits at 5% per stock
   - Configure stop-loss at 2% portfolio drawdown
   - Test kill-switch functionality

3. **Performance Monitoring**
   - Track daily P&L calculations
   - Monitor Sharpe ratio improvements
   - Validate performance fee calculations (30% of profits)

### Phase 2: Small Capital Live Trading ($1,000-$5,000)
**Goal**: Validate with real money, minimal risk

**Strategy**: Conservative quantum-enhanced approach
1. **Diversified Quantum Portfolio**
   - Use VQE for sector allocation (25% tech, 25% finance, 25% healthcare, 25% energy)
   - Apply QAOA for individual stock selection within sectors
   - Quantum ML for entry/exit timing

2. **Performance Tracking**
   - Document all trades and quantum algorithm recommendations
   - Compare against S&P 500 benchmark
   - Calculate actual vs projected performance fees

### Phase 3: Scaling Based on Results
**Goal**: Scale successful strategies

**If profitable after 30 days**: Increase capital to $10,000-$25,000
**If underperforming**: Refine algorithms and continue paper trading
