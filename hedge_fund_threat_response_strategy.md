- Analyze their exploration patterns to understand competitor gaps

- Gather evidence of institutional validation for sales materials

## Strategic Response Framework

### Phase 1: Observation & Data Collection (24-48 hours)
**Passive Monitoring:**
- Allow continued access while logging everything
- Track algorithmic sophistication of their probes
- Monitor for team coordination (multiple IPs, user agents)
- Assess if they're evaluating AlphaForge for acquisition or competition

### Phase 2: Selective Information Control
**Gradual Restriction:**
- Begin serving outdated or less sophisticated algorithm examples
- Limit access to quantum optimization details
- Show "coming soon" for most advanced features
- Redirect sophisticated queries to basic educational content

### Phase 3: Active Countermeasures (if warranted)
**Protective Actions:**
- IP-based restrictions for repeated canary triggers
- CAPTCHAs for suspected automated behavior
- Rate limiting specifically for institutional signatures
- Legal notice serving for terms of service violations
